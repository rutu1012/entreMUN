About Entre MUN

Entrepreneurship Cell,VNIT in collaboration with Competitve Exam Club Samvād , also the Debate and Discussion club, VNIT brings to you Entre-MUN , an extravagant event planned for the Global e-summit of our premier fest Consortium-’22. This MUN will present a golden opportunity to students to help flourish their diplomacy and debating skills on contemporary global crises! It is a simulation of the proceedings of a United Nations meet, which further aids students in manifesting their leadership and public speaking adroitness!!

About Us

E-Cell

E-Cell VNIT is the entrepreneurship cell of VNIT The very purpose of E-CEll is to foster a community of entrepreneurs and stimulate a constructive ecosystem for them. Throughout the year E-cell organises events which promtoes managerial value amongbudding students across the country.

CEC Samvād

CEC Samvād is the Debate and Discussion club along with being the Competitve Exam Club of VNIT. Having such a wide jurisdiction has allowed us to carefully curate and envision a platform that allows for students to safely communicate their views , methodologies and improve their oratory skills through the forums of discussion and debates happening in great jest throughout the year. Sprinkled along the way are our Guest and Student Talks that allow our students to inculcate necessary first hand exposure to further guide them and achieve greater heights.
